## 2. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### 2.1 How do I change my mosque?
You can change your mosque by tapping the mosque name at the top of the **Home** page. This will open a map where you can select a different mosque. Your preferences will be saved automatically.

### 2.2 Why am I not receiving prayer notifications?
- Ensure that you have granted notification permissions in your device settings.
- Check the **Notification Settings** in the app's settings menu to ensure prayer alerts are toggled ON.
- Some Android devices have battery optimization settings that can delay notifications. Ensure Mosalla is set to "Don't optimize" or "Allow background activity."

### 2.3 The prayer times are different from my local mosque.
Prayer times are provided and managed by the mosque administrators. If you notice a discrepancy, please contact the administrators of that specific mosque (Student associations Mehnat or MSS).

### 2.4 How do I find the Qibla?
Tap the **Qibla** tab in the bottom navigation bar. Ensure you are away from large metal objects or magnetic sources for the most accurate reading. You may need to calibrate your compass by moving your phone in a figure-8 motion.
